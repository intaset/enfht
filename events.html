Exhibits

There are currently two types of opportunities for an exhibit at ENFHT. They are as followed (prices are in Euros):

Table exhibit, €1500:

  • A table setup for the entire duration of the conference
  • 1 free registration for the personnel from the institute
  • An additional €300.00 for each accompanying person

Booth exhibit, €1500:

  • A booth setup for 1 conference day
  • 1 free registration for the personnel from the institute
  • An additional €300.00 for each accompanying person

The exhibit provides a platform for companies and institutions to present their products and distribute brochures and business cards. The exhibit also provides a unique networking opportunity with many experts and researchers in the field of combustion science and processes.

If you are interested in an exhibit at CSP, please do not hesitate to contact us at info@enfht.com.
